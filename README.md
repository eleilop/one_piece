<h3>🚀 1. Hero Section</h3>
<img src="./img-readme/hero.png" alt="Hero section" style="width:50%">
<p>I have used a background image of Luffy's crew that easily adapts to different devices.<br>Above it I have placed a Call to Action that directs us to the Synopsis section.</p>

<h3>📖 2. Synopsis</h3>
<img src="./img-readme/synopsis.png" alt="Synopsis section" style="width:50%">
<p>The Synopsis section provides a complete and concise narrative of the storyline.<br>From Monkey D. Luffy's humble beginnings to even the explanation of his curious abilities,<br>this section will be the essential guide for newcomers and a refreshing review for veterans.</p>

<h3>👤 3. Characters</h3>
<img src="./img-readme/characters.png" alt="Characters section" style="width:50%">
<p>Discover more about the fascinating characters that bring One Piece to life.<br>Each profile will provide detailed information about the character's history,<br>abilities, and contributions to the storyline.</p>

<h3>⚔️ 4. Battles</h3>
<img src="./img-readme/battles.png" alt="Battles section section" style="width:50%">
<p>The Battles section will highlight the most epic and thrilling showdowns in One Piece.<br>From Luffy's fights to duels between powerful warriors, this area will offer detailed<br>videos from the bests confrontations.</p>

<h3>🖼️ 5. Gallery</h3>
<img src="./img-readme/gallery.png" alt="Gallery section" style="width:50%">
<p>Explore the rich collection of images and artwork from One Piece in the Gallery.<br>From official illustrations to outstanding fan art, this section is a visual feast for fans.</p>

<h3>👣 6. Footer</h3>
<img src="./img-readme/footer.png" alt="Footer section" style="width:50%">
<p>Follow the official One Piece social networks so you don't miss the last detail.<br>From here you can access the credits section.</p>
